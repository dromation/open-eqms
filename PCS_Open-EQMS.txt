Open-EQMS is designed to be customizable and flexible, allowing organizations to tailor the system to their specific needs and requirements. The software is designed to be user-friendly, with an intuitive interface and a range of features to support quality management processes, including document management, corrective and preventive actions, and audit management.

Open-EQMS is compatible with a range of operating systems and can be installed on-premises or hosted in the cloud. The software is designed to be scalable, supporting organizations of all sizes, from small businesses to large enterprises.
